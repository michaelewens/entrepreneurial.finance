This module aims to answer the following questions:

1. What drives a private firm to go public?
2. What happens to firms after they go public?

## Required and Suggested Readings

We will cover two papers and the institutional details required to understand them. Read these two papers and skim the articles on the week 6 reading list.

### Paper 1

Ewens, M. and Farre-Mensa, J. (2020) 'The Deregulation of the Private Equity Markets and the Decline in IPOs', *The Review of Financial Studies*, 33(12), pp. 5463-5509.

### Paper 2

Bernstein, S. (2015) 'Does going public affect innovation?', *The Journal of Finance*, 70(4), pp. 1365-1403.
