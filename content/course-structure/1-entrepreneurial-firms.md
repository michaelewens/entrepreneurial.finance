In the first module, we will answer the following questions:

1. What is the entrepreneurial firm (in this class)?
2. How do these firms differ from their non-entrepreneurial peers?
3. What are the major research questions in entrepreneurial finance?
4. What do we know about the entrepreneurial entry decision?

## Required and Suggested Readings

We will cover two papers and the institutional details required to understand them. Read these two papers and skim the articles on the module 1 reading list.

### Paper 1

Hurst, E. and Pugsley, B.W. (2011) 'What Do Small Businesses Do?', *Brookings Papers on Economic Activity*, 2011(2), pp. 73-118. Available at: https://doi.org/10.1353/eca.2011.0017

### Paper 2

Levine, R. and Rubinstein, Y. (2017) 'Smart and Illicit: Who Becomes an Entrepreneur and Do They Earn More?', *The Quarterly Journal of Economics*, 132(2), pp. 963-1018. Available at: https://doi.org/10.1093/qje/qjw044
